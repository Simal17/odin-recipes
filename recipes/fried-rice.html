Fried Rice

Description

Authentic Chinese fried rice with Chinese sausage, eggs and steamed rice. This restaurant style Chinese fried rice recipe is so easy to make and the best!

This recipe is from Rasa Malaysia

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Chinese sausage, or lap cheong
  • Eggs
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Steamed rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Ground white pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and chill the rice overnight in the fridge.
  2. Heat up a wok or skillet on high heat. Add cooking oil and stir fry the garlic, follow by the Chinese sausage.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and rice to the wok. Stir and toss all the ingredients around before adding the soy sauce and pepper.
  4. Push the rice to one side of the wok, add some oil to cook the eggs. When the eggs are set, break them up, combine, and toss well with the rice.
